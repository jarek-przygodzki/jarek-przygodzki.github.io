About me

Welcome on my official website. I’m Jarek Przygódzki, software engineer with 10+ years of professional software development experience living in Poznań, Poland. I am a Kotlin/Scala/Go/Rust enthusiast, but primarily use Java for my job. I like efficient code, fixing things that are broken, building things that work and knowing how things really work.

This blog is a collection of my thoughts on various topics in professional software development. I plan to write here about interesting things that I encounter, stuff I've coded, and whatever I feel like writing about.

You can expect to find various cool posts on programming/devops. I also work in Java, so that may show up here and there, too.
